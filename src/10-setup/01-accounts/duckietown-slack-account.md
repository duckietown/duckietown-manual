# Joining the Duckietown Slack

Duckietown has a large community of instructors, learners and professionals on Slack. All Duckietown community-related communications are published through the #00-general channel there.

* [Join the Duckietown community on Slack](https://duckietown.com/join-slack).

Joining the Duckietown community is a way to get support too. Learn [how to get help in Duckietown](how-to-get-help) in the FAQs and troubleshooting section of this book.
