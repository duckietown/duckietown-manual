# Getting Student Feedback

In our experience, it is well worth the effort to get detailed feedback from your students at regular intervals during the course. We solicit more detailed feedback than our standard University feedback forms.

Here is [an example Google form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/12joDyTAIr_kS-jFuMz30nk2QeGyQUjM8lhH9yuBOego/edit) used to solicit feedback on all aspects of the course at its completion.
