# Visual Perception

This section lists slides and recordings used to present topics related to visual perception.

This includes a definition of visual perception tasks, an introduction to neural and deep convolutional neural networks, and a more thorough investigation of the problems of object detection and place recognition (which is related to [SLAM](slides-and-recordings-estimation-slam)).

A prerequisite for understanding this material could be the basics of [](slides-and-recordings-vision-overview). Much of this material is supported by the [learning experience](learning-experiences) related to "Object Detection".
