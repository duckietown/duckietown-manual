# Estimating Uncertainty

With the inclusion of more deep-learning-based models inside autonomous vehicle systems, the issue of [safety](slides-and-recordings-safety) becomes even more central, but also challenging.

One hope is to build deep learning models that are also able to output *calibrated* estimates of their confidence. This requires understanding the different types and sources of uncertainty in the output of deep-learning model.
