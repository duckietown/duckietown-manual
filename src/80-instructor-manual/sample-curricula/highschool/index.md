# High School level

Duckietown is designed for university-level education, so there are no readily available curricula we offer at this point. 

Nonetheless, several high schools have used Duckietown.  

If you are interested in exploring the possibility of using Duckietown in your class, robotics club, or summer camp, please [reach out to us](https://duckietown.com/contact/) and we will be happy to discuss your particular case.
