# Who Should Read This?

This resource is intended to be a resource for people who are looking to **build** a class that uses the Duckietown platform in some way.

In other words, this is designed for:

* **teachers**, instructors or professors;
* **teaching assistants** (TAs) may also find the content here useful.

We recommend the materials specifically for **university or college** classes either at the **graduate** or **undergraduate** level.

It is not impossible to use Duckietown at high school or lower age/skill levels, but a key consideration in that case would be to think about the [prerequisites](considerations-prerequisites).

You also need not be developing a class on *robot autonomy* necessarily. The platform can be used as a component of a course centered on **control systems**, **computer vision**, **planning**, **autonomy**, and many other topics.
